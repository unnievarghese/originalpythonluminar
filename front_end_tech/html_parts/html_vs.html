Langer rallies behind Paine, Smith amid criticism over SCG conduct

Justin Langer, the Australian head coach, has backed the captain Tim Paine following the incidents at SCG Test, which ended in a thrilling draw. Through the course of the third match of the ongoing Border-Gavaskar Trophy, the Australian captain made some unsavoury comments directed at R Ashwin and even the umpires, for which he later apologised. It was far removed from the conduct Australia had been attempting in their bid for a changed image of their cricket team following the Sandpapergate.

Opportunities galore as Root's England embark on 17-Test journey

It was Albert Einstein who said, "In the middle of difficulty lies opportunity". He almost certainly wasn't thinking of England's Test side at the time but his words resonate with the position Joe Root's team find themselves in ahead of a two match series against Sri Lanka which begins a momentous, exacting year of Test cricket. The road ahead for England is clearly difficult, but opportunities, for individuals and the collective, are there for the taking.
